About me

I am Yentl Stroobants, a 21-year old Applied Computer Science student at Thomas More Geel, Belgium. In my free time I like to socialize with friends and just do things together with them.

Applied Computer Science

In secondary school I studied Human Sciences. I didn't really knew what I wanted to do afterwards. I always liked the political part of Human Sciences but in my free time I was always working with computers. At the end of secondary school I had a teacher who suggested me to study IT because it was one of my best courses and I really enjoyed it. I was just afraid that it would be too much of a challenge because I studied Human Sciences. Nevertheless I started studying applied computer science and to this day I am still happy I started it .

Artificial Intelligence

In my third year I have chosen for the specialization Artificial Intelligence because I like to work with new technologies that will be used in the future. In this specialization we learned not only to analyse data and visualise it, but we also learned to build neural networks and to handle large chunks of data.

Application Development

After Artificial Intelligence I chose to also enroll in the specialization Application Development. This would give me a wider knowledge of IT. During the first semester I learned to use new frameworks to create applications like Angular and React. But I think I learned the most with advanced programming topics where we learned to use docker and github actions properly. The frameworks were also a nice foundation to use new frameworks later on.
