Project 4.0

During Project 4.0 We worked together with students from different specializations. The team consisted of 1 student who studied IOT, a student who studied cloud and cyber security, 2 students who studied application development and 2 students who studied artificial intelligence. The objective was to create a proof of concept for a smart bin with a website to monitor it. Next to that we also created a system to take surveys and visualise the answers. This was a project for the city of Herentals, Belgium. They wanted to create a smart city and we created the foundations for it. My part was mostly to create the BI dashboards in Qlik. After that we integrated these BI dashboards in our website with JavaScript. We managed our data in Microsoft Azure.

What did I learn

During this project I learned to work together with people that studied other things. We didn't have a lot of knowledge about the things the other students where making but we learned to put it all together into a working project. Next to that we learned to create something for someone outside of school and start from the ground up.
